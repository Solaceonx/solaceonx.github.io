Free application help

I've been lucky enough to be accepted to Stanford as a Stanford Graduate Fellow (one of four in this year's incoming physics cohort) and a KIPAC Fellow, and to receive the Goldwater Scholarship and an NSF Graduate Research Fellowship along the way. None of that happened on my own — mentors gave up their time to read my drafts and talk things through, and I'd genuinely like to pass that forward.

This is mostly aimed at rising juniors getting ready to apply to graduate school, but if you happen to have found this page, the offer stands for you too. I'm glad to read over your graduate school or fellowship materials, talk through strategy, or just grab a coffee chat to answer questions, completely free of charge. I can't promise to get to every request, so I work through them roughly first-come, first-served, but I'll always do my best to make time.
